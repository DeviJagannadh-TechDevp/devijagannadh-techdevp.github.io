Dr. Surekha Kagitapu

Artist & Dentist

Hello, I'm Dr. Surekha Kagitapu, a passionate dentist and dedicated artist. Beyond the confines of the dental clinic, I find solace and joy in the world of art. My love for drawing has been a constant companion, providing me with a creative outlet and a means of self-expression. As a dentist, I am committed to promoting oral health and ensuring smiles that radiate confidence.

Simultaneously, my artistic endeavors allow me to explore the beauty of form and color, creating a harmonious balance in my life. Whether crafting beautiful smiles or translating emotions onto canvas, my journey is a fusion of science and art, both of which contribute to the tapestry of my identity. Join me in celebrating the artistry of dentistry and the joy of creating on this colorful canvas we call life.
